Google vs the Human Condition

Aug 19th, 2015

Google's co-founder Larry Page recently announced the reorganizing of Google as Alphabet, and it's mission to fight some of mankind's most basic ailments.

We all know Google, as the billionaire tech giant who dominates internet searches. What many may not realize is they are much much more. From their own programming language Go to self-driving cars, Google seems to dominate technology. The industry goliath, with all their brilliant scientists and engineers now turn their focus on the prevention of disease.

While that may come as a surprise to some, this has really been a long time coming. Ever hear of Google X? Google X is a facility of research that encompasses Artificial Intelligence, Computational neuroscience, Health care and Biotechnology and was founded back in 2010. The Life Sciences division at Google X has been researching medicine for some time now. Under Alphabet, problems with human affliction will no longer just be a side project. It will be their guiding mission.
